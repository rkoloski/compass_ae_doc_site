Engine Details: Base ERP Services


The erp_base_erp_services engine seems redundantly named because of the strong desire to have a common prefix for all of the erp framework engines that comprise CompassAE’s foundation.

The very start of the tutorials that became CompassAE were implementations of object-relational mappings for Business Parties, Roles and RoleTypes, Party Relationships and the basic demographic and contact information that typically refers to Individuals and Organizations.
