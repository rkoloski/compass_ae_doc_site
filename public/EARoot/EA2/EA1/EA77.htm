<b>Money </b>(or MoneyAmount) is an <b>Amount </b>of a specific <b>Currency</b>. Instead of classes like <b>Payments </b>or <b>FinancialTxns</b> holding the amounts directly, they hold references to an instance of <b>Money</b>. An alternate design would be to factor <b>Currency</b>, but leave the Amounts in the individual classes where they are used.
