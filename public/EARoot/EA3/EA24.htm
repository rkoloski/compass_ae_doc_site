<b>CompassAE Implementation Patterns</b><br /></p><p><br /></p><p>This section describes in detail some implementation patterns that are used throughout the CompassAE framework. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>It is a central design principle in CompassAE that the system use repeating patterns and conventions wherever practical. This is more akin to the “principle of least surprise” than “convention over configuration,” since the goal is to facilitate the process of familiarization rather than to avoid configuration complexity.<br /></p><p><b>How classes are extended incrementally in CompassERP</b><br /></p><p><br /></p><p>Many large-scale technology platforms are distributed in several smaller, separately installable (and separately license-able) modules. With a business processing framework like CompassAE, this creates the challenge of how to incrementally add functionality to the platform at an individual component level.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>We could just replace the class, but it’s nicer to be able to compose just the functionality we want without having to have a file for each possible combination of functions.<br /></p><p><br /></p>
